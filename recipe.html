How to prepare paneer ?

Ingredients

  • 250 grams paneer (2 heaped cups)
  • 1 tbsp Oil.
  • 1 cup onions cubed, 3 medium (optional)
  • 1½ cups tomatoes finely chopped (3 large)
  • salt as needed.
  • 10 cashew nuts.
  • 1½ tbsp Butter.



      • Steps

      1. Heat a pan with oil.
      2. Next add tomatoes and fry them with salt for 3 minutes.
      3. Add garam masala , red chilly powder and sugar if needed.
      4. Transfer paneer butter masala with garlic to a serving bowl and then garnish with creem.
